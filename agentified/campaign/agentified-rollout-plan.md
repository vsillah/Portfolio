## Campaign intent

Agentified should enter the market through one practical claim:

Product leaders cannot scale agentic work on speed alone. They scale it through trust.

The campaign should make the book feel like the next step after "Accelerated": the SAM loop learned how to move work, and Agentified shows how to govern that movement when agents, memory, approvals, receipts, and operating systems enter the picture.

## Review standard

Before any item moves into Portfolio's Social Content calendar as actionable content:

- The asset must have a named campaign phase, channel, and scheduled date.
- The draft must stay public-safe: no private chats, raw manuscript source, client data, secrets, or unapproved inferences.
- Any reference to "Accelerated" should preserve the lineage without making Agentified sound dependent on the first book.
- Any system claim must be framed as Vambah's operating model or case study unless independently sourced.
- The human gate must remain `pending` until Vambah explicitly approves the item.
