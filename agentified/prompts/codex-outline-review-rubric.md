# Codex Review Rubric: Agentified Expanded Outline

Use this after Fable 5 generates:

- `/Users/vambahsillah/Projects/Portfolio/agentified/drafts/agentified-expanded-outline-fable5-v1.md`

Review goal: push the outline until it is structurally as strong as, or stronger than, `Accelerated`, and sounds like Vambah.

## Review Dimensions

Score each dimension 1 to 5.

### 1. Sequel Fit

Question: Does this clearly build from `Accelerated`?

Look for:

- Sam's learning-system arc continues naturally.
- Product principles from `Accelerated` are upgraded into agentic OS principles.
- The book does not become a generic agent trend commentary.

Reject if:

- It could be written by anyone with a few AI agent buzzwords.
- It drops the prior book's product-management spine.

### 2. Structural Strength

Question: Is the architecture at least as strong as `Accelerated`?

Look for:

- Three clear acts.
- Escalating stakes.
- Strong act turns.
- Chapter-level progression.
- Each chapter has a concrete job.
- Final chapters create a satisfying before/after.

Reject if:

- The outline is a list of topics without narrative movement.
- Chapters repeat the same point with different labels.

### 3. Storytelling Strength

Question: Does Sam's story carry the concepts?

Look for:

- Each chapter opens with a real work tension.
- Sam faces tradeoffs, pressure, near misses, and decisions.
- The plot evolves from learning system to governed agentic operating system.

Reject if:

- Sam is decorative.
- The outline reads like a white paper.

### 4. Vambah Voice

Question: Does it sound like Vambah's grounded, practical, morally clear style?

Look for:

- Plain strong sentences.
- Concrete scenes.
- Systems diagnosis.
- Practical path forward.
- Moral clarity without preaching.

Reject if:

- It uses generic AI hype.
- It leans on consulting filler.
- It overuses formulaic antithesis or stock "future of work" language.

### 5. Portfolio Proof

Question: Does the outline use what Vambah actually built?

Look for:

- Agent Ops.
- Open Brain.
- Shaka.
- Mission Control.
- approval gates.
- trace/run artifacts.
- Mobile App Foundry.
- Model Ops AutoResearch.
- client-safe governance exports.

Reject if:

- Examples are generic or disconnected from Portfolio.
- Claims overstate autonomy or production authority.

### 6. Privacy And Source Safety

Question: Does it keep private material safe?

Look for:

- Structural examples rather than raw private traces.
- Public-safe phrasing.
- Clear approval gates before real client examples, screenshots, or trace excerpts.

Reject if:

- It quotes private chats, client records, raw traces, secrets, or sensitive personal data.

### 7. Framework Quality

Question: Are the frameworks memorable and usable?

Look for:

- Clear names.
- Simple components.
- Diagram/table potential.
- Practical reader exercises.

Reject if:

- Frameworks feel invented for decoration.
- They do not help the reader build or evaluate an agentic system.

## Review Output Format

Write review notes to:

- `/Users/vambahsillah/Projects/Portfolio/agentified/reviews/agentified-expanded-outline-codex-review-v1.md`

Use this structure:

1. `Codex review receipt`
2. Overall recommendation: `accept`, `revise`, or `reject`
3. Score table
4. Top 10 required revisions
5. Chapter-level notes
6. Voice and language notes
7. Privacy/source-safety notes
8. Recommended Fable 5 revision prompt
9. Decision gates for Vambah

## Minimum Bar To Accept

Do not accept the outline unless:

- Sequel Fit >= 4
- Structural Strength >= 4
- Storytelling Strength >= 4
- Vambah Voice >= 4
- Portfolio Proof >= 4
- Privacy And Source Safety = 5
- Framework Quality >= 4
