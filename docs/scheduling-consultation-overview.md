# Scheduling and Consultation Overview

Use this section to answer questions like "How do I book a call?", "Can I schedule a consultation?", "What happens on a discovery call?", "What meetings happen after I buy?", or "How do we get started?"

## First Step for Prospects

The normal first step is a free Discovery Call.

| Meeting | Duration | Purpose | Booking |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Discovery Call | 30 minutes | Talk through the visitor's situation, goals, constraints, and whether an AI automation project is worth pursuing. This is not meant to be a hard pitch. | https://calendly.com/amadutown/atas-discovery-call |

When someone asks to schedule, book a consultation, talk to someone, discuss services, or figure out which package is right for them, direct them to the Discovery Call link:

https://calendly.com/amadutown/atas-discovery-call

## What Happens on the Discovery Call

The Discovery Call is used to understand:

- The business problem or opportunity the visitor wants to address.
- Current tools, workflows, and constraints.
- Whether a lightweight AI audit, a strategy workshop, or a deployed automation package is the right next step.
- Whether the visitor is a fit for Quick Win, Accelerator, Growth Engine, Digital Transformation, or a custom path.

If the visitor has already completed the AI Audit, the call can use those results to personalize recommendations and success criteria.

## Meetings After a Client Commits

After a client purchases or commits to an engagement, later meetings may include:

| Meeting | Duration | Purpose |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Onboarding Call | 45 minutes | Intake, credentials, tooling access, communication cadence, and next steps. |
| Kick Off Meeting | 60 minutes | Align scope, timeline, deliverables, team roles, and project risks before implementation starts. |
| Progress Check-in | 30 minutes | Review what shipped, what is blocked, and what needs to be adjusted. |
| Delivery & Review | 45 minutes | Walk through delivered work, validate against scope, gather feedback, and confirm next steps. |
| Renewal Review | 30 minutes | Review outcomes and decide whether to continue, expand, or hand off cleanly. |

For public website visitors, do not expose internal scheduling links unless they are already provided. Use the Discovery Call link as the default.

## How to Answer Scheduling Questions

- If the visitor is asking to speak, schedule, book, meet, call, or discuss fit, give the Discovery Call link.
- If the visitor asks what happens before buying, explain the Discovery Call and AI Audit path.
- If the visitor asks what happens after buying, summarize onboarding, kickoff, progress check-ins, delivery review, and renewal review.
- If the visitor is unsure which package fits, recommend the Discovery Call and, when relevant, the free AI Audit.
- Keep scheduling answers short and action-oriented.
