# Agentic Content Research Briefs

This folder contains the research execution layer for the agentic content series.

The PRDs in `docs/agentic-content-research-prds/` define what to research. These briefs turn that backlog into source-backed content material that can become LinkedIn posts, carousels, talks, and Phase 2 YouTube scripts.

## Operating Rule

Research is allowed to shape public claims. It should not publish, send, generate avatar video, regenerate audio, or mutate production workflows.

Public-facing content should separate three things:

- What the broader market is doing.
- What Portfolio can prove through local code, docs, traces, and admin surfaces.
- What remains an opinion, recommendation, or next hardening step.
